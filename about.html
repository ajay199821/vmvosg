About Us

Veera Marthanda Varma Open Scout Group (41st Kuttanad) is an open group registered under Kerala State Bharat Scouts & Guides. Our group registered on 2018 with group of senior scouts in the Kuttanad district association. The aim of VMVOSG is social services and development of group members with specific objectives. The group actively organises many service activities and programs within the movement and outside the movement.
