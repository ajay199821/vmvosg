Scheme of AdvancementScout

Motto: "Be Prepared"

Scout Promise

“On my honour, I promise that I will do my best To do my duty to God* and my country, To help other people and To obey the Scout Law’

* The word ‘Dharma’ may be substituted for the word “God” if so desired.

Cub Law

(i) A Scout is Trustworthy.
(ii) A Scout is loyal.
(iii) A Scout is a friend to all and a brother to every other Scout.
(iv) A Scout is courteous.
(v) A Scout is a friend to animals and loves nature.
(vi) A Scout is disciplined and helps protect public property.
(vii) A Scout is courageous.
(viii) A Scout is thrifty.
(ix) A Scout is pure in thought, word and deed.




A boy who is a citizen of India and who as completed 10 years but not completed 17 years of age is eligible to be become a Scout Aspirant. A Scout Aspirant shall complete Pravesh requirement and will work for three months to the satisfaction of the Scout Master before being invested as a Scout.

*If a Scout continues to be in the School he should be allowed to remain in the unit till he completes 18 years of age.

Having worked for at least three months and attended four Troop Meetings a Scout Aspirant can be invested as a Scout on completion of Pravesh Test.

Pravesh Scout

A Pravesh Scout will work at least for six months to qualify for Pratham Sopan.

Pratham Sopan Scout

A Pratham Sopan Scout will work for at least six months to qualify for Dwitiya Sopan.

Dwitiya Sopan Scout

A Dwitiya Sopan Scout will work for at least six months to qualify for Tritiya Sopan.

Tritiya Sopan Scout

A Tritiya Sopan Scout will work for at least nine months to qualify for Rajya Puraskar Badge.

Rajya Puraskar Scout

Work for atleast 6 months (common for both Wings) and completed 13 yrs. On completion of the requirements and testing, Rajya Puraskar [Badge and Certificate signed by the Governor] is awarded by the Governor on suitable occasion.

Rashtrapati Scout Award

Work for at least 12 months and completed 14 yrsRashtrapati Award: Work for at least 9 months On completion of the requirements, Regional Testing camp will be organized. The Award [Badge and Certificate signed by the President] is presented by the President of India at Rashtrapati Bhavan on a suitable occasion.
