Scheme of AdvancementCub

Motto: "Do Your Best"

Cub Promise

“I promise to do my best To do my duty to God* and my country To keep the Cub Law and To do a Good-turn every day.”

* The word ‘Dharma’ may be substituted for the word “God” if so desired.

Cub Law

(i) A Cub is obedient.
(ii) A Cub is clean and polite.
Cub Aspirant

A Boy can be registered as a Cub Aspirant on completion of five years of age and invested after working for at least three months and completed the requirements of the Pravesh test.

Pravesh Cub

A Pravesh Cub will work at least for three months to qualify for Pratham Charan.

Pratham Charan Cub

A Pravesh Cub will work at least for three months to qualify for Pratham Charan.

Dwitiya Charan Cub

A Dwitiya Charan Cub will work for at least nine months to qualify for Tritiya Charan badge.

Tritiya Charan Cub

A Boy can be registered as a Cub Aspirant on completion of five years of age and invested after working for at least three months and completed the requirements of the Pravesh test.

Chaturtha Charan Cub

A Boy can be registered as a Cub Aspirant on completion of five years of age and invested after working for at least three months and completed the requirements of the Pravesh test.
